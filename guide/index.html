Beginner’s Guide (Spoiler-Light)

Understand the core loop and make intentional choices. Use this as a companion for your first runs.

Core loop

Balance short-term needs with long-term consequences. Small efficiencies accumulate; tiny mistakes can cascade.

  • Read prompts carefully; wording often hints at thresholds.
  • Keep a simple log of choices and results for 2–3 cycles.
  • Avoid committing too early; leave room to pivot.

First 2–3 runs (plan)

  1. Play it safe for orientation; stabilize metrics first.
  2. On the next run, change one decision at a time.
  3. On the third run, try a deliberate opposite route.

Common mistakes

  • Chasing two goals at once in the same window.
  • Ignoring the weakest metric until it cascades.
  • Repeating a failed sequence without changing order.

Accessibility & controls

  • Use Fullscreen (guide) for readability.
  • Keyboard: F toggles fullscreen; Esc exits.
  • Mobile: rotate to landscape; zoom if text feels small.

FAQ

Is it free to play?

Yes. You can play in your browser with no downloads.

Does it save progress?

The web version is designed for short sessions; progress may not persist between reloads.

Is there a best first route?

Start compliance-leaning to learn systems, then try a defiance route.

How long is a run?

Short sessions are common; treat early attempts as scouting.
