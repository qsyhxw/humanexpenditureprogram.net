How to Play in Fullscreen

Click the fullscreen button, double-click, or press F on your keyboard.

Play in your browser Open fallback

Fullscreen methods (3 ways)

  1. Click the fullscreen button in the player controls.
  2. Double-click the player area.
  3. Press F on your keyboard; press Esc to exit.

Keyboard shortcuts and browser tips

  • F toggles fullscreen; Esc exits.
  • Use landscape on mobile; rotate if needed.
  • Zoom 110–125% if text feels small.

Performance tips

  • Close heavy background tabs and streams.
  • Prefer Wi-Fi over mobile data when possible.
  • Use the latest Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

FAQ

How do I enter fullscreen?

Click the fullscreen button, double-click the player, or press F.

How do I exit fullscreen?

Press Esc or click the fullscreen button again.

Why does the game not start in fullscreen?

To keep pages fast and stable; click fullscreen when you are ready.

Does fullscreen improve performance?

Fullscreen improves readability; performance depends on your device and network.
